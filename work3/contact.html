FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?+
We accept STL, OBJ, STEP, IGES, 3MF, and AMF files. If you only have a 2D drawing or sketch, our design team can create a 3D model for you starting at $50.
How long does an order take?+
FDM parts can ship in as little as 24 hours. SLA typically takes 2–4 days, and SLS 4–7 days. Express options are available for an additional fee. Production runs are quoted individually.
What is your minimum order quantity?+
There is no minimum order quantity. We print single units for prototyping and scale up to thousands for production runs, with volume pricing kicking in at 50+ units.
Do you ship internationally?+
Yes — we ship worldwide. Standard international delivery takes 5–10 business days. Express international shipping is available. Free shipping on orders over $200 within the US.
What accuracy can I expect?+
FDM achieves ±0.2mm, SLA achieves ±0.05mm, and SLS achieves ±0.1mm. For critical tolerances, we recommend SLA. All parts undergo dimensional inspection before shipping.
Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?+
Yes. If a part doesn't match the approved design specifications, we will reprint or refund it. Custom designs and client-provided files are final once approved, but we work with you to get it right.
