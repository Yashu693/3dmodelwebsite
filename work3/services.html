Post-Processing

Finishing That
Makes the Difference

Every part we ship has been through quality inspection. Add finishing options to take it further.

🖌️
Sanding & Priming

Manual sanding through progressive grits (80→2000) followed by high-build primer. Creates a paint-ready, smooth surface.

🎨
Painting & Finishing

Custom RAL or Pantone color matching. Matte, satin, or gloss clear coats for durability and visual impact.

✨
Vapor Smoothing

Acetone or isopropyl vapor treatment for ABS and ASA. Creates injection-mold-quality surfaces in one step.

🔧
Thread Inserts

Heat-set brass threaded inserts installed for durable, re-usable mechanical connections without the need for glue.

⚡
Electroplating

Chrome, nickel, or copper plating for a premium metallic appearance. Also improves electrical conductivity.

📐
Assembly & Testing

We assemble multi-part designs, install hardware, and perform functional testing before shipping.
